Miso Ramen

Description

Miso ramen is a beautiful dish. The miso marries the flavors of salt and creaminess and the weight is comforting and an absolute classic for cold weather. In fact, miso ramen comes from the Northern Hokkaido region of Japan - where bitter winters blow in and call for a warming bowl of something heavy, robust, and ready to set you up to face the day. Miso ramen is the youngest of the four main ramen types but itâ€™s hugely popular. Full of umami flavors, high in protein, and awesome for an immune boost, there are so many reasons to love miso ramen.

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles 200g
  • Pork (sliced or minced) 100g
  • Boiled Egg 1
  • Bean Sprouts 100g
  • Green Onion 30g
  • Bamboo Shoots 100g
  • Corn 50g
  • Miso 4 tbsp
  • Chicken Soup Broth 1 tbsp
  • Water 800ml
  • Sesame Oil 1 tbsp
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a pan and cook pork and bean sprouts with salt and pepper.
  2. Put all the ingredients for Ramen Soup and warm it in a pot.
  3. Boil ramen in another pot.
  4. Place the soup and ramen in a bowl with [1] and other ingredients on the top.
